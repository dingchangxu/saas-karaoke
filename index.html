How It Works

Here’s a quick rundown of online services where you can find quality indie music. SingOn’s song catalogue has something for everyone and you can enjoy new songs every week. No more need for discs as you can access all songs instantly.

Download Music

Forget the expensive karaoke devices and discs, now you have it on your web browser. Just log into from any computer or your smartpone. Download any music with one click. Hand-pick single files from preview files from cloud.

Singer Nearby

The Singer Nearby service is a serverless communication service: Singer nearby can help you find new singer nearby! Locate fellow users on the map and engage them in exciting conversations. It helps you find new friends!

Singing Online

Choose the songs you want to sing and add them to the song queue even when someone is singing. Record your versions of your favorite songs and share them with the world!

Find Music

The infinite catalog of music, new and old, is a both a blessing and a curse. View, find and add more songs to the singing queue with the free SingOn mobile app while others are singing.

Follow

Follow your favorite singer, Share and record your performance. Enjoy thousands of songs ranging from evergreen classics to the latest billboard hits!
